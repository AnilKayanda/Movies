Ayla

The film will bring to the screen a true and very dramatic story that took place in the Korean War. Süleyman Sergeant, who took part in the war in 1950, finds a little girl on the battlefield. This 5-year-old Korean girl is an orphan and does not know where to go. The petty officer takes the girl with him and names her Ayla. Ayla, who has become the joy of the union, and the petty officer soon become like father and daughter. However, at the end of 15 months, the union decided to return to Turkey. Suleyman Petty Officer, who does not want to leave Ayla and return, can not go beyond the Korean laws, even though he tries every way. Süleyman, who had to leave the little girl behind, and Ayla, who will be given to the orphanage by being included in the system applied to the orphans, promise to meet again in their last goodbyes. Will the years bring the two together again?
